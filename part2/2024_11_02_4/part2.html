Shaxian snacks have a long and rich history, originating from the Chinese culinary culture in the Yellow River Basin during the Xia, Shang, Zhou, Jin, and Song dynasties.
Shaxian snacks are the noodle culture of Hakka immigrants more than 1000 years ago, which is a product of the fusion with the rice culture of the Minyue ethnic group in Sanming, Fujian.
The food culture of the Central Plains spread to Shaxian with the repeated southward migration of the Central Plains people. One of the main reasons why Shaxian's snacks are famous
